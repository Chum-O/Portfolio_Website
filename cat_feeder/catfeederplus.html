Automated Cat Feeder

The Automated Cat Feeder (Plus)

A group of students and I are currently making an automated cat feeder using a Raspberry Pi that connects to a mobile app, allowing users to set feed times and even customize the size of the feed. It also has a fun laser toy module with an attached camera, letting users check-in on and play with their cats from anywhere!


Materials

  • Raspberry Pi 4 Model B
  • High Torque DC Motor
  • DC & Stepper Motor Pi Bonnet
  • LCD Display
  • PWM Motor Pi Bonnet
  • Dual Servo Pan-Tilt Kit
  • TTL Laser Diode
  • Raspberry Pi Camera Module
  • Power Supply
  • Cereal dispenser
  • GPIO Riser Pack
